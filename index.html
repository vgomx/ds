VG Design System

Component Library · 16 cards · v0.1

Brand

Masthead lockup Wordmark + meta on an ink rule — how the brand signs a page
The VG symbol A folded diamond monogram — the only mark, on paper and ink

Colors

Ink → paper ramp The cool neutral gray ramp — every surface and text tone lives here
Accent — monochrome + reserved green Ink is the default accent; green is opt-in via [data-accent=green]
Semantic + destructive Status and the single reserved red, in light and dark tones

Spacing

Edges, not shadows Surfaces separate by hairline borders and scrims — drop shadows reserved only for free-floating overlays
Radius — 2px is the only softness One radius for every surface; circles only for dots and avatars
Spacing scale A 4/8 base; section rhythm runs generous — 48 / 80

Type

Archivo — display scale Hero, section heading and card title — tight, grotesque, confident
Archivo — body & lead Lead paragraph and body copy at their working sizes
JetBrains Mono — the labelling voice Eyebrows, data and section markers — uppercase and tracked

Components

Core — Buttons, Tags, Badges, Input Actions, labels, status and fields
Feedback — Progress, Spinner, Skeleton, Accordion Loading, progress and disclosure
Navigation — Breadcrumb, Pagination, Stepper Wayfinding and progress through a process
Overlay — Menu, Modal, Drawer Dropdowns and scrimmed surfaces
Data — Cards, Stats, Table Surfaces, figures and the index table
